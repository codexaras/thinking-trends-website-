🛒 E-commerce

D2C Fashion Brand — Revenue Scale

Scaling a fashion brand from ₹3L/month to ₹18L/month via Meta Ads.

6×Revenue growth
3.4×ROAS
−42%Cost per buy

The problem

Great products but poor ad structure, no retargeting, and a website converting at under 1%. CAC was unsustainably high.

Our strategy

Full funnel rebuild: UGC-style creatives for cold audiences, dynamic product ads for warm, email sequences for retention. Landing-page CRO doubled conversion rates.

The results

Revenue grew from ₹3L → ₹18L/month in 90 days. ROAS stabilized at 3.4×. Acquisition cost dropped 42%.

Boutique Resort — Brand & Bookings

Building an Instagram presence that converted into direct bookings.

0→28KInstagram followers
+180%Direct bookings

The problem

A beautiful resort with 200 followers, zero engagement, and full dependence on OTA platforms taking 25% commission.
